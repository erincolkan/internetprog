Curious Case of Benjamin Button

Curious Case of Benjamin Button is a movie made in 2009, directed by David Fincher and
starring Brad Pitt and Cate Blanchett. The storyline is loosely based on the 1922 short story
of the same name by F. Scott Fitzgerald.Benjamin has a disease, he was aging the opposite of normal people.
His mother died when she was giving birth to him and his father abandoned him because of the anger of his wife's
death and the sadness when he saw Benjamin's old, ugly face. Where he abandoned him was a nursing home and the
woman responsible from this place raised Benjamin. The lesson I learned and why I love this movie is because the
"The Ugly Duckling" like story. You shouldn't judge people by their appereances and anybody who is a ugly duckling
can turn into a gorgeous swan. This isn't just a basic ethical lesson, it's a very big and important metaphor if
you can understand. And the second thing I like in this movie is the timeless, beautiful and unsparing love among
Benjamin and Daisy. No matter where they go, no matter they have been through,no matter they were old or young
the fate and their love for each other always kept them connected. This is a kind of love that
everyone wants but only someone can have.
