Hugh Laurie - Kiss of Fire

Actually this song is written by Angel Villoldo and originally
named "El Choclo". Many recorded reprises of this song, notably
Louis Armstrong and Nat King Cole. But other reprisers didn't include
original lyrics as Hugh Laurie did. English parts are exact translations
of the Spanish parts.
